WHAT IS SCUBA HUNT?

An exciting and unique experience in the realm of underwater adventure. You earn the merit and win by deciphering puzzling codes.

A problem-solving dive, which is part scavenger hunt, part escape room, part puzzle, and lots of fun.

The course has a unique scenario made up of 90 huge concrete blocks. It's your job to follow the clues and figure out the code to unlock the treasure box in 70 minutes.

THE GAME

ScubaHunt is a one tank dive, so can easily fit in among other dives on your trip, or even can be completed on that final day before flying, as the maximum depth is only 19ft.

WHO PLAYS

WHO PLAYS

ScubaHunt is for certified divers who are looking for an unconventional and thrilling adventure.

Divers need to have confidence in their diving skills to be able to focus on navigating around the puzzles and not worry about buoyancy. They should also be comfortable with a possible strong current.

CONVENIENT LOCATION

CONVENIENT LOCATION

The game is located at the iconic beachfront Hemingway Restaurant, just a five-minute taxi ride north of the ferry terminal.

It is the perfect location to chill out after the game: have some drinks, good food, enjoy the ocean view, and watch the sunset.

SAFETY FIRST

SAFETY FIRST

All the guides are experienced PADI Dive Masters and EFR and First Aid trained.

The whole course is protected and delimited by safety buoys markers to ensure that no boat crosses the area.
