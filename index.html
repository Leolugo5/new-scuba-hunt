WHAT IS SCUBA HUNT?

An exciting and unique experience in the realm of underwater adventure. You earn the merit and win by deciphering puzzling codes.

A problem-solving dive, which is part scavenger hunt, part escape room, part puzzle, and lots of fun.

The course has a unique scenario made up of 90 huge concrete blocks. It's your job to follow the clues and figure out the code to unlock the treasure box in 70 minutes.

THE GAME

ScubaHunt is a one tank dive, so can easily fit in among other dives on your trip, or even can be completed on that final day before flying, as the maximum depth is only 19ft.
